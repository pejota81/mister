# Mister v0.15 — News System, Dashboard Polish & Save Reliability

> Released: 2026-05-03

This release introduces a full in-game news system that surfaces events from across the football world, alongside a redesigned Dashboard, a unified post-match screen, and several stability and UX improvements.

---

## News System

A live news feed is now available front-and-centre on the Dashboard. News is generated automatically by the game engine as events happen and is cleared at the start of each new season.

### News categories

| Category | Coverage |
|----------|----------|
| **Competition** | Cup results (your matches + round summaries); season-end champions, promotions & relegations per division |
| **People** | Retirements (grouped by division); youth breakthroughs (grouped by division); managerial changes |
| **Club** | Board / fan sentiment after runs of form; Manager of the Month |
| **Finance** | Packed-house attendance highlights; budget warnings |
| **Performance** | Player goal milestones; contract expiry alerts |

### Tabbed news panel

The news panel on the Dashboard is split into six tabs:

- **All** — every item newest first
- **Competition** — league news grouped by division, cup news in its own section
- **People** — retirements and youth breakthroughs combined, one item per division listing all players (name · club · age)
- **Club** — sentiment and manager award headlines
- **Finance** — attendance and budget alerts
- **Performance** — goal milestones and contract warnings

Each tab shows an unread count badge. Selecting an item marks it as read.

### Detail panel

Clicking any news item opens a detail panel that shows:

- Full headline with category colour accent bar
- Club, division, player (age / skill / goals), and manager info cards
- For multi-player items (retirements, youth, contract alerts): a structured table listing every player's name, club, and age
- Dismiss button

### News lifecycle

- Season-start news (retirements, youth, contract alerts) is generated when a new season begins
- Weekly news (sentiment, milestones, cup results, attendance) is generated after each week is advanced
- Season-end news (titles, promotions, relegations, managerial changes) is generated before the new season rolls over
- **All news is cleared at the start of every new season** — no inbox overflow

## Dashboard Improvements

### Fixtures & Results panel

The former separate "Next Fixture" and "Recent Results" panels have been merged into a single **Fixtures & Results** panel:

- Consistent row layout for both upcoming and past matches (badge · teams + competition · week indicator)
- Upcoming matches: `NOW` pill for this week, `W{n}` badge for future weeks
- Past results: W / D / L coloured badge
- Away indicator (✈) on the competition sub-line
- "Skip Week" renamed to "Advance to Week N"

### League table

The Dashboard mini-table now matches the post-match screen style:

- Top 5 clubs + `···` separator row + your position when you are outside the top 5
- Colour-coded goal difference (green positive, red negative)
- Managed club row highlighted and shown in amber
- W / D / L columns hidden on small screens (`col-hide-sm`)
- Panel title shows the league name: *📊 {League} — Standings*

## Post-Match Screen

- **Continue button moved to the top** of the screen so you no longer need to scroll past results, stats, and the league table to navigate back to the Dashboard

## Save System Improvements

- **Auto-retry** — after a save failure, the game automatically retries after a 20-second cooldown
- **Manual Retry button** — a "Retry" button appears in the NavBar when a save error is shown, allowing an immediate retry
- **Error auto-clear** — the "⚠ Save failed" message automatically clears after 8 seconds instead of persisting indefinitely
- **Debounce** — rapid game actions are batched; saves are committed after a short quiet period to avoid overlapping requests

---

## Transfers — Budget Display Fix

The Transfers page previously showed the club's cash balance labelled as "Budget", while the Finances page labelled the same value "Balance" and separately tracked the board-allocated Transfer Budget.

Both pages now use consistent terminology:

- **Balance** — total club cash on hand
- **Transfer Budget** — board-allocated spending cap (shown as *remaining / total*, highlighted red if breached)

The "within budget" filter in the Transfers market now checks **both** cash balance and remaining transfer budget allowance.
