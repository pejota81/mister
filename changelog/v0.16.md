# Mister v0.16 — Dashboard Layout Refresh, Top Scorers & News Actions

> Released: 2026-05-16

This release improves the main dashboard flow and upgrades the news experience with better navigation and direct gameplay actions.

---

## Dashboard Layout Refresh

The dashboard row order was updated to prioritize operational information:

- **Row 1:** Club Overview + Fixtures & Results
- **Row 2:** Latest News (full-width)
- **Row 3:** Standings (left) + Top Scorers (right)

This makes core team status and match context immediately visible while keeping news central and readable.

---

## New Top Scorers Panel

A new **Top Scorers** table now appears on the dashboard (row 3, right):

- Scorers are filtered to the managed club's current division
- Sorted by season goals
- Shows up to 8 players
- Highlights players from your own club
- Uses compact columns for small screens

---

## News Experience Improvements

### Keyboard navigation

You can now navigate news items with keyboard arrows in the focused news list:

- **Arrow Down:** next item
- **Arrow Up:** previous item

### Direct actions from Performance news

Performance-related news now includes quick actions in the detail panel, allowing immediate navigation to relevant screens:

- **Open Squad**
- **Open Transfers** (contract-related items)
- **Open Tactics** (other performance items)

---

## Mobile UX

The new dashboard composition and news interactions were tuned to remain clean and usable on mobile layouts.
