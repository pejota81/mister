# Mister v0.09 — Tactics Overhaul & Player Stats

> Released: 2026-05-02

---

## Tactics Screen Revamp

The Tactics screen has been significantly expanded to give you full control over your lineup before every match.

### Mandatory Tactics Review

The match simulation is no longer triggered directly from the Dashboard or the Match Day screen.

- **Dashboard** → "Simulate Week" now navigates to the Tactics screen first
- **Match Day** → "Next Week" also routes through Tactics
- A new **▶ CONFIRM & SIMULATE** button on the Tactics screen saves the lineup and kicks off the simulation in one click
- The existing **SAVE LINEUP** button remains for saving and browsing other screens before simulating

### 2 New Formations

| Formation | Character | ATK mod | DEF mod |
|-----------|-----------|---------|---------|
| `4-2-3-1` | Double pivot — balanced edge on both sides | ×1.07 | ×1.05 |
| `4-1-4-1` | Deep single pivot — defensive block | ×0.88 | ×1.15 |

### Mentality-Aware 4-Segment Formations

For formations with 4 numeric segments (`4-2-3-1`, `4-1-4-1`), the two middle groups are now interpreted differently based on your chosen mentality:

| Mentality | 2nd group | 3rd group | Example (`4-2-3-1`) |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|----------------------|
| Defensive | DEF | MID | 1 GK · 6 DEF · 3 MID · 1 FWD |
| Balanced  | MID | MID | 1 GK · 4 DEF · 5 MID · 1 FWD |
| Attacking | MID | FWD | 1 GK · 4 DEF · 2 MID · 4 FWD |

Changing mentality while Auto mode is active immediately re-selects the XI.

### Auto / Manual Selection Mode

A new **Selection Mode** panel on the Tactics screen lets you choose how the starting XI is built.

**Auto (⚡)**
- Fills the XI immediately, picking the best player by Form score for each position slot required by the current formation
- Switching formation or mentality re-runs the selection automatically
- A hint shows the slot breakdown: e.g. `Best by Form: 1 GK · 4 DEF · 4 MID · 2 FWD`

**Manual (✏️)**
- Clears the current selection so you start fresh
- Click a player row to cycle: **None → Starting XI → Substitute → None**
- No modifier keys needed (replaces the Ctrl+Click mechanic that did not work on macOS)

No players are selected by default when starting a new session.

### Substitutes Expanded

The substitute bench has been increased from **5** to **12** slots.

## Squad Table — New Columns

The squad table in the Tactics screen now shows:

| Column | Description | Colour coding |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| **Fit** | Current fitness (0–100) — depletes after matches | 🟢 ≥70 · 🟡 40–69 · 🔴 <40 |
| **Stm** | Stamina (0–100) — recovery speed and performance consistency | 🟢 ≥70 · 🟡 40–69 · 🔴 <40 |
| **Form** | `round(skill × (0.6 + 0.4 × fitness/100))` — the pre-variance effective skill the match engine uses | Bold |
| **Gls** | Goals scored this season | |
| **Ast** | Assists this season | |

**Form** is the most actionable number: it reflects exactly what the simulation uses before random variance kicks in. A player with Skill 80 and Fitness 50 has Form 64 — the same impact as a fresh Skill 64 player.

## Player Stats — Goals & Assists Tracking

Goals and assists are now tracked per player across seasons.

### Season Stats

- `seasonGoals` and `seasonAssists` are incremented after every simulated match
- Stats accumulate for all players whose club played that week (not just the managed club)
- Both counters reset to 0 at the start of each new season

### Career Stats

- `careerGoals` and `careerAssists` are permanent counters that never reset
- At each season rollover, season totals are added into career totals before being cleared
- Newly generated players (youth, free agents) start at 0 and build their records over time

### Assists Logic

Approximately **75% of goals** are attributed an assist to a second attacking player. The remaining 25% are recorded as solo goals (no assist credited).

## Auto-Select Fills the Full Bench

When **Auto** selection mode is active, the bench is now populated in addition to the starting XI.

- After filling the 11 starters, the remaining squad players are ranked by Form score and the best 12 are automatically added as substitutes
- The bench re-populates whenever formation or mentality changes, just like the XI does
- Previously, Auto mode only set the starting XI and left the bench empty

Two new sortable columns have been added to the Squad table, visible after the **Fit** column:

| Column | Shows |
|--------|-------|

## Contract Length Picker on Signing

Every time you sign a player — whether a free agent or a contracted transfer — you now choose the contract duration before confirming.

### How It Works

Clicking **Sign** in the Transfer Market no longer immediately triggers a confirm dialog. Instead, an inline contract picker appears in the same row:

1. A dropdown lists the available season options based on the player's age
2. Select the desired length and click **✓** to complete the signing, or **✕** to cancel
3. The player's contract is set to exactly the chosen duration, overriding any previous contract they held

### Contract Length Limits by Age

| Age bracket | Maximum contract |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Under 25 | 1–5 seasons |
| 26–32 | 1–3 seasons |
| 33+ | 1 season only |

The picker defaults to 2 seasons (or the maximum if lower than 2).

### Free Agent Contract Bug Fix

Free agents previously showed **0 seasons** on their contract after being signed because their `contractSeasonsLeft` was never updated in the signing action. This is now always set from the chosen contract length.

## Contract Duration Picker on Signing

Every time you sign a player — free agent or contracted transfer — you now choose the contract length before confirming.

### How it works

1. Click **Sign** in the Transfer Market → the button is replaced inline with a season dropdown and ✓ / ✕ buttons
2. Select the desired contract length
3. Click **✓** to confirm — the player joins with exactly that contract duration

The chosen length overrides the player's previous contract, so contracted players arriving from other clubs start fresh with the term you set.

### Maximum contract by age

| Age band | Max contract |
|----------|-------------|
| Under 25 | 5 seasons |
| 26 – 32  | 3 seasons |
| 33+      | 1 season only |

The dropdown only shows valid options for the player's age — it is not possible to offer an over-age contract.

### Default selection

The picker defaults to **2 seasons** (or the age-band maximum if that is lower than 2).
