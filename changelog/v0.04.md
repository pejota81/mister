> Released: 2026-05-02

## Cup Competition Overhaul

The cup competition has been completely redesigned with a proper two-phase structure that reflects real football knockout tournaments.

### Structure

- **B League Qualifying** (rounds 1–4): 63 B-league clubs enter a single-leg bracket. The bracket is seeded to 64 with one bye for the top seed, producing 4 qualifying rounds. Matches use extra time + penalties if level after 90 minutes.
- **Combined Phase** (rounds 5–8 — Round of 16, Quarter-Finals, Semi-Finals, Final): All 16 remaining clubs (4 B qualifiers + 12 A-league representatives) compete in **two-legged ties**. The aggregate score decides the winner; level aggregate triggers extra time and then penalties on the second leg.

### Calendar Integration

| Phase | Weeks | Format |
|-------|-------|--------|
| B Qualifying Round 1 | Week 3 | Single leg |
| B Qualifying Round 2 | Week 6 | Single leg |
| B Qualifying Round 3 | Week 10 | Single leg |
| B Qualifying Round 4 | Week 14 | Single leg |
| Round of 16 | Weeks 17 & 21 | 2 legs |
| Quarter-Finals | Weeks 25 & 29 | 2 legs |
| Semi-Finals | Weeks 33 & 37 | 2 legs |
| Final | Weeks 41 & 45 | 2 legs |

### Cup Screen

- Two distinct sections: **B League Qualifying** and **Combined Phase**
- Qualifying shows standard match rows (home vs away, score, ET/PEN badges)
- Combined phase groups each tie with both leg scores and the running aggregate
- Leg 1 and Leg 2 week info shown per tie
- ET and penalty shootout results displayed on the decisive leg

## Season Calendar Redesign

All divisions now operate on a **fixed 46-week season** — no more trailing ghost weeks.

### League Types

| Type | Clubs | Match Rounds | Ghost Weeks |
|------|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **A leagues** (England PL, Brazil, Italy, Spain) | 20 | 38 | 8 cup weeks |
| **B leagues** (Germany, France, Portugal, Netherlands) | 18 | 34 | 8 cup weeks + 4 B qualifying weeks |
| **Championship** (England 2nd tier) | 24 | 46 | None — plays every week |

- A and B leagues idle during cup weeks; B leagues also idle during their 4 qualifying weeks
- Week assignment is now explicit via constants — no more dynamic calculations

### League Reordering

- **Netherlands** moved to rank 8 (was 9)
- **Championship** moved to rank 9 (was 8)
- Championship clubs are **not eligible for the Cup** while in Division 9 — promotion restores cup eligibility

### Promotion & Relegation Boundary Fixes

| Division | Rank | Promotion | Relegation |
|----------|------|-----------|------------|
| Premier League | 1 | 0 (top — nowhere to go up) | 3 |
| Netherlands (Eredivisie) | 8 | 3 → Portugal | 3 → Championship |
| Championship (England) | 9 | 3 → Netherlands | 0 (bottom — nowhere to go down) |

- Fixed Championship having `relegationSpots: 3` — it is the last division, there is nothing below it
- Fixed Netherlands having `relegationSpots: 0` — now correctly relegates 3 clubs to the Championship

## Country Code Badges

With promotions and relegations mixing clubs from different countries, all club names now display their **3-letter international country code** (e.g. `ENG`, `BRA`, `NED`, `ESP`) as a subtle suffix.

- **Table screen** — code shown after every club name in the standings
- **Cup screen** — code shown next to the shortname in both qualifying match rows and 2-legged tie displays

The code is derived at data-load time from the club's division → league → `countryCode` chain, so it follows the club wherever they are promoted or relegated to.

## Bug Fixes

### Game Data Cache Busting

After deploying data updates, browsers and the Azure CDN were serving a stale `gamedata.json`, causing players to see outdated league names and division info even after a hard refresh.

Two fixes applied:
- **`staticwebapp.config.json`** — added `Cache-Control: no-cache, no-store, must-revalidate` for all `/data/*.json` files, preventing CDN and browser caching
- **`loader.ts`** — the fetch request now uses `{ cache: 'no-cache' }` and appends a `?v=` timestamp query string as an additional bypass layer

### CI/CD Pipeline Overwriting League Ranks (Root Cause)

Every push to `main` triggers `azure-static-web-apps.yml`, which runs `scripts/fetch-leagues.mjs` to regenerate `gamedata.json` before deploying. This script still had the **old ranks** (Championship=8, Netherlands=9), so it was silently overwriting the corrected JSON files on every single deploy — making the rank swap completely ineffective in production.

**Fix:** Swapped the ranks in `fetch-leagues.mjs`:
- `DED` (Eredivisie / Netherlands): `iffhsRank: 8`
- `ELC` (Championship / England): `iffhsRank: 9`
