# PJfoot v0.04 — Cup Competition & Calendar Redesign

> Released: 2026-05-02

---

## Cup Competition Overhaul

The cup competition has been completely redesigned with a proper two-phase structure that reflects real football knockout tournaments.

### Structure

- **B League Qualifying** (rounds 1–4): 63 B-league clubs enter a single-leg bracket. The bracket is seeded to 64 with one bye for the top seed, producing 4 qualifying rounds. Matches use extra time + penalties if level after 90 minutes.
- **Combined Phase** (rounds 5–8 — Round of 16, Quarter-Finals, Semi-Finals, Final): All 16 remaining clubs (4 B qualifiers + 12 A-league representatives) compete in **two-legged ties**. The aggregate score decides the winner; level aggregate triggers extra time and then penalties on the second leg.

### Calendar Integration

| Phase | Weeks | Format |
|-------|-------|--------|
| B Qualifying Round 1 | Week 3 | Single leg |
| B Qualifying Round 2 | Week 6 | Single leg |
| B Qualifying Round 3 | Week 10 | Single leg |
| B Qualifying Round 4 | Week 14 | Single leg |
| Round of 16 | Weeks 17 & 21 | 2 legs |
| Quarter-Finals | Weeks 25 & 29 | 2 legs |
| Semi-Finals | Weeks 33 & 37 | 2 legs |
| Final | Weeks 41 & 45 | 2 legs |

### Cup Screen

- Two distinct sections: **B League Qualifying** and **Combined Phase**
- Qualifying shows standard match rows (home vs away, score, ET/PEN badges)
- Combined phase groups each tie with both leg scores and the running aggregate
- Leg 1 and Leg 2 week info shown per tie
- ET and penalty shootout results displayed on the decisive leg

## Season Calendar Redesign

All divisions now operate on a **fixed 46-week season** — no more trailing ghost weeks.

### League Types

| Type | Clubs | Match Rounds | Ghost Weeks |
|------|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **A leagues** (England PL, Brazil, Italy, Spain) | 20 | 38 | 8 cup weeks |
| **B leagues** (Germany, France, Portugal, Netherlands) | 18 | 34 | 8 cup weeks + 4 B qualifying weeks |
| **Championship** (England 2nd tier) | 24 | 46 | None — plays every week |

- A and B leagues idle during cup weeks; B leagues also idle during their 4 qualifying weeks
- Week assignment is now explicit via constants — no more dynamic calculations

### League Reordering

- **Netherlands** moved to rank 8 (was 9)
- **Championship** moved to rank 9 (was 8)
- Championship clubs are **not eligible for the Cup** while in Division 9 — promotion restores cup eligibility
