# Mister v0.08 — Transfer Market Overhaul & Free Agency

> Released: 2026-05-02

---

## Transfer Windows

Contracted players can now only move during official transfer windows. Free agents remain signable at any time.

- **Summer window** — weeks 1–5 (season start)
- **Winter window** — weeks 20–23 (mid-season)
- A coloured banner in the Transfer Market screen shows whether a window is open or closed
- Contracted players are hidden from the market when no window is active

---

## AI Transfer Listings

When a transfer window opens, AI clubs automatically list surplus and ageing players for sale.

### Criteria for Listing

An AI club will mark a player as available if any of the following apply:

- Age ≥ 32
- Contract expires at end of season
- Player is in the bottom 20% of the club's squad by skill
- Random 10% churn (any player may be listed)

Up to 4 players per club are listed per window. All listings are cleared when the window closes.

---

## AI Offers Inbox

AI clubs can now make bids for your listed players.

- Offers arrive each week while a transfer window is open
- Each offer displays the bidding club, fee, and expiry week
- **Accept** — player transfers immediately, fee added to your balance
- **Reject** — offer is removed; the club may bid again
- Offers auto-expire after 2 weeks if unanswered
- Access your inbox via the **Incoming Offers** tab in the Transfer Market screen

---

## Loan System

You can now loan players out to AI clubs for half a season (19 weeks).

- Loan fee is paid upfront (10× weekly salary)
- Loaned players show a **LOAN** badge in the Squad screen and cannot be toggled for sale while on loan
- Players automatically return to your squad when the loan expires

---

## Advanced Filters

The Transfer Market browse screen now includes:

- **Min skill** slider
- **Max age** filter
- **Max salary/wk** filter
- **Free agents only** toggle
- Name and position filters (unchanged)

## Free Agency Overhaul

### Starting Free-Agent Pool

On a new game, **80 synthetic free agents** (age 23–34, skill 60–85) are injected into the market so there are interesting options immediately available.

### AI Signing Rules

- AI clubs in the **top 5 divisions** (global IFFHS rank ≤ 5) do **not** sign free agents — they use the paid transfer market only
- Lower-division AI clubs start signing free agents after **week 5** of each season (giving the player a 5-week head start)
- A second signing pass runs at **week 20** (start of winter window period)
- AI aggression ramps up over 4 seasons: season 1 is very passive (signs only if squad < 12), reaching full aggression by season 4 (signs if squad < 22)

### Player Free-Agent Limit

- The player-managed club can sign a maximum of **5 free agents per season**
- The remaining slots are displayed in the Transfer Market banner as **FA Slots: X/5**
- The Sign button is disabled (with a tooltip) when the limit is reached
- The counter resets at the start of each new season

---

## Contract Expiry & Retirement Fixes

- Players whose contracts expire at season end are now immediately released as **free agents** (previously they remained at their club)
- Mandatory retirements (age > 38) are now replaced **1-for-1** with 18-year-old youth players — no cap
- Youth players are also added in the **first season** to replace mandatory retirements
- Free agents who reach retirement age are removed from the player pool during season aging

---

## Breaking Change — Saved Games

Existing saves load correctly. Two new fields are auto-migrated:

- `season.pendingOffers` — defaults to `[]`
- `season.freeAgentsSigned` — defaults to `0`

No data loss; old saves continue working without a new game.

## Season Budget Reset

Transfer and wage budgets are now recalculated at the start of every season based on the club's actual financial situation.

### Transfer Budget

The board now allocates the **higher** of:

- **15% of expected season income** (prize money + matchday) — the board-floor allocation
- **30% of current club balance** — rewards clubs that have accumulated wealth

A club that has saved up from a good run of results will have a meaningfully larger transfer war chest next season.

### Wage Budget

Unchanged — calculated as **70% of expected season income ÷ total weeks**. This reflects the club's ability to sustain ongoing wages, not its cash reserves.

Both counters (`transferSpent`) reset to zero at the start of each new season.

## Squad Capacity Expansion

The maximum squad size has been increased to accommodate a full reserve team.

- **Team A** (first squad): up to **30 players** (unchanged)
- **Team B** (reserves): up to **15 players** (was unlimited)
- **Total**: 45 players across both squads
- Squad headers now show capacity as `X/30` and `X/15`
- The "→ B" button is disabled with a tooltip when Team B is full

## Transfer Market Improvements

### Budget Filter

A **"Within my budget"** checkbox has been added to the market filters, enabled by default. It hides players the club cannot afford, so the sign button is always active for visible players (unless another rule blocks it).

### Sortable Columns

All columns in the market table are now sortable by clicking the header:

- **Name, Pos, Nat, Club** — sort alphabetically (ascending by default)
- **Age, Skill, Value, Salary** — sort numerically (descending by default)
- Click the same column again to reverse direction; an ▲▼ arrow shows the active sort

### Sign Button Tooltips

The Sign button now shows a tooltip explaining exactly why it is disabled:

- `Not enough funds (balance: $X, fee: $Y)`
- `Squad is full (30 players max)`

## Transfer & Finance Rule Fixes

Several inconsistencies in how transfers are recorded and how money flows have been corrected.

### Free Agents Always Cost $0

The `BUY_PLAYER` action now derives the fee internally: `$0` for free agents, market value for contracted players. This is no longer a value the UI can accidentally pass incorrectly.

- No balance is deducted when signing a free agent
- Free agent signings never count against `transferSpent` or trigger a budget-breach penalty
- The `negotiable` flag is now correctly cleared when a player is signed

### Ledger Now Updated Immediately

`season.ledger.transfersSpent` was previously only updated as a weekly mirror of `club.seasonBudget.transferSpent`, causing it to be one week behind and potentially missing the last transfer of the season.

All three transfer actions now update the ledger directly:

| Action | Ledger effect |
|---|---|
| `BUY_PLAYER` (contracted) | `+fee` added to `transfersSpent` |
| `BUY_PLAYER` (free agent) | No change (fee is $0) |
| `ACCEPT_TRANSFER_OFFER` (sale) | `−fee` reduces `transfersSpent` (sale income offsets spending) |
| `ACCEPT_TRANSFER_OFFER` (loan-out) | `−fee` reduces `transfersSpent` |
| `LOAN_OUT_PLAYER` | `−fee` reduces `transfersSpent` |

### Dead Code Removed

A `void newTransferSpent` statement in the permanent-sale path was calculating but discarding the updated transfer budget. The sale income is now correctly reflected in the ledger.

## Transfer Market Improvements

### "Within My Budget" Filter

A **Within my budget** checkbox (enabled by default) hides players whose transfer fee exceeds your current balance. This keeps the market list clean and relevant — only players you can actually sign are shown. You can uncheck it to browse freely.

### Sortable Columns

All columns in the market table are now sortable. Click any column header to sort:

- **Name, Position, Nationality** — alphabetical
- **Age, Skill, Market Value, Salary** — numeric
- The active sort column shows a ▲ or ▼ indicator
- Clicking the same column toggles between ascending and descending

### Disabled Button Tooltips

When the Sign button is greyed out, hovering it now shows the exact reason:

- *Not enough funds (balance: $X, fee: $Y)*
- *Squad is full (30 players max)*
- *Transfer window closed*
- *Free-agent signing limit reached for this season*

## Transfer Rules Consistency Fixes

Several bugs in the transfer and financial logic were found and corrected.

### Free Agent Fee is Always $0

The `BUY_PLAYER` action now derives the transfer fee internally — free agents always cost **$0**, regardless of their market value. The fee is never trusted from the UI call. Previously, the market value was being used as the fee for free agents, which could incorrectly block signings.

### Ledger Updates are Immediate

`season.ledger.transfersSpent` is now updated the moment a transfer occurs, not just mirrored during the weekly advance. This means the Finances screen immediately reflects purchases.

### Sale and Loan Proceeds Offset Net Spending

When you accept a permanent offer or loan out a player, the incoming fee now reduces `ledger.transfersSpent` (sale income offsets what you spent). This gives an accurate net transfer balance for the season.

### Dead Code Removed

A `void newTransferSpent` line in the permanent-sale path was silently discarding the calculated budget update. This has been removed and the correct behaviour applied.

### `negotiable` Flag Always Cleared on Purchase

When buying a player, their `negotiable` flag is now always cleared (previously this was missed in the `BUY_PLAYER` reducer).
