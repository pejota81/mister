# Mister v0.06 — Finance & Stadium Overhaul

> Released: 2026-05-02

---

## Prize Money

League and cup prize money has been completely redesigned. Every club now earns something — higher finishes pay more, lower divisions pay less.

### League Prize Pools

Prize pools are distributed across **all clubs** in a division using linear positional decay — 1st place earns the highest share, last place the lowest, but nobody walks away empty-handed.

| Division | Prize Pool |
|----------|-----------|
| Premier League | $250M |
| Série A (Brazil) | $200M |
| Serie A (Italy) | $175M |
| Primera División | $150M |
| Bundesliga | $125M |
| Ligue 1 | $100M |
| Primeira Liga | $75M |

### Cup Prize Money

Every team earns prize money when eliminated (or on winning the final):

| Round | Loser receives | Winner receives |

## Ticket Prices & Attendance

Ticket pricing and stadium attendance are now fully **automatic and momentum-driven** — no manual input required.

### Division-Based Price Ranges

Each division has a minimum and maximum ticket price. The actual price scales linearly with manager momentum (0 → min price, 100 → max price):

| Division | Min | Max |
|----------|-----|-----|
| Premier League | $50 | $100 |
| Série A / Serie A | $40 | $90 |
| Primera División | $35 | $85 |
| Bundesliga | $30 | $70 |
| Ligue 1 | $28 | $68 |
| Primeira Liga / Eredivisie | $25 | $65 |
| Championship | $15 | $45 |

### Attendance

- Ranges from **30% to 100%** of stadium capacity
- Formula: `attendancePct = 30% + (momentum / 100) × 70%`
- High momentum → packed stadium; low momentum → quiet ground

### Home Advantage

Attendance now affects match results. A packed stadium gives your team a boost:

- Base home multiplier: **1.05×**
- Maximum (100% attendance): **1.12×**
- Formula: `boost = 1.05 + ((attendancePct − 0.30) / 0.70) × 0.07`

## Stadium Expansion

Stadium expansion now uses a **three-tier system** with build time and division capacity caps.

### Expansion Options

| Option | Capacity Gain | Build Time | Cost per Seat |
|--------|--------------|------------|---------------|
| Small | +10% | 2 weeks | avg ticket × 6 |
| Medium | +25% | 4 weeks | avg ticket × 7 |
| Large | +50% | 8 weeks | avg ticket × 8 |

- Cost is deducted immediately; new capacity is added when construction completes
- A 🏗 crane indicator appears on the stadium visual during construction

### Division Capacity Caps

| Divisions | Max Capacity |
|-----------|-------------|
| Ranks 1–4 (top 4 leagues) | 120,000 |
| Ranks 5–6 | 100,000 |
| Ranks 7–8 | 80,000 |
| Rank 9 (Championship) | 70,000 |

### Stadium Visual

The Finances screen now includes an **SVG stadium diagram** showing:
- Current crowd fill level (proportional to expected attendance)
- Capacity bar vs division cap
- Construction crane overlay when an expansion is in progress

## Bank Loans

The loan system has been redesigned as a **season-bridge financing** model.

### How It Works

- Loans come in **3 tiers** sized relative to the division's 1st-place prize
- No weekly repayments — the full amount (principal + interest) is automatically deducted at **season end**
- Only one active loan at a time

### Loan Tiers

| Tier | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Small | 25% of 1st-place prize |
| Medium | 50% of 1st-place prize |
| Large | 100% of 1st-place prize |

### Interest

- **0.75% per week** accrued from the week the loan is taken to the end of the season
- The Finances screen shows the projected total repayment at the time of borrowing

### Bankruptcy

If your club's balance goes **negative at any moment** — whether from wages, expansion costs, or loan repayment at season end — **you are immediately fired** with a bankruptcy-specific message.

## Season Budget

The board now allocates a **season budget** at the start of each season, automatically computed from your division's expected income.

### Transfer Budget
- Set to **15% of expected season income** (mid-table prize + matchday projection)
- Tracks cumulative transfer spending with a progress bar
- **Soft limit:** exceeding the transfer budget applies a −3 momentum penalty per purchase

### Wage Budget
- Maximum **weekly wage bill** the board is comfortable with
- Set to **70% of weekly expected income**
- Same soft −3 momentum penalty if breached on contract renewal

## Season Forecast

A **Season Forecast** panel now projects your end-of-season balance based on:
- Expected prize money at your **current table position**
- Remaining matchday revenue (home games left × momentum-driven attendance & price)
- Remaining wage costs
- Loan repayment (if applicable)

## Financial Summary — Weekly / Season Tabs

The financial summary panel has been redesigned with a **tab toggle**:

### "This Week" tab
- Balance (large, prominent — red if negative)
- Weekly wages
- Matchday revenue — shows estimate if home game this week, "No home game" otherwise
- Net this week
- Financial health bar (green/amber/red based on projected end balance)

### "This Season" tab
- Matchday earned (exact, accumulated week by week)
- Prize money received (league + cup, exact)
- Wages paid (exact)
- Transfers spent
- Season net

The **season ledger** is tracked precisely throughout the season and reset at each new season start.

---

## Breaking Change — Saved Games

Existing saves from v0.05 will load, but:

- `loanAmount` / `loanWeeklyRepayment` on clubs are now deprecated — they are ignored by the new finance engine. Active loans use the new `activeLoan` field.
- `season.ledger` will be missing from old saves — it is initialised to zeros safely on load.
- `club.seasonBudget` will be missing from old saves — budgets are recomputed at the next season start.

**Recommendation:** start a new game to experience the full finance system from season 1.
