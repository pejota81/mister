# MISTER v0.27.3 - Mirrored Second-Half Fixtures

> Released: 2026-05-17

Patch release focused on enforcing strict mirrored rematches in league fixtures.

---

## Fixes

- **Second-half mirroring:** the second half of the season now follows the exact same round order as the first half, with home/away swapped for every match.
- **Mid-season balance preserved:** first-half home/away balance constraints remain enforced so each team stays within a maximum difference of 1 game.

## Technical

- Updated `src/engine/fixtures.ts`:
  - Removed independent second-leg round reordering.
  - Second-leg rounds are now generated directly from first-leg rounds by flipping venues.
  - Kept first-half feasibility guards and candidate scoring for home/away balance.
